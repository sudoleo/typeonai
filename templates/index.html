FAQ & Quick Start

What is consens.io?
consens.io instantly queries multiple leading AI models, creating a unified "consensus" answer. It also integrates real-time web search for accurate, up-to-date results.

Benefits:
✓ Reliable consensus answers
✓ Real-time web search
✓ Easy query bookmarking

1. 🤖 What is Auto Consensus? ＋

Auto Consensus automatically combines and compares answers from multiple AI models, highlighting differences, correcting errors, and providing a reliable final response.

2. 🔘 What is the Consensus Button? ＋

The Consensus Button generates a unified answer by merging responses from selected models. You can include (✅) or exclude (❌) specific model outputs before clicking the button. It ensures that only trusted answers influence the final result.

3. 🧠 What is the Consensus Model? ＋

The Consensus Model is a special AI that processes and synthesizes responses from other models. It analyzes agreement, corrects inconsistencies, and creates a coherent final answer based on the selected responses.

4. 🧩 What are Differences? ＋

Differences highlight conflicting or unique parts across model responses. This lets you quickly spot where models disagree or contribute distinct perspectives—helping you make informed decisions before generating a consensus.

5. 💡 What is Deep Think Mode? ＋

Deep Think Mode provides an in-depth, step-by-step reasoning from selected AI models, offering detailed explanations and deeper insights into complex queries.

6. 🌐 What is Web Search? ＋

Web Search sends your query to GPT, Gemini, Exa (an AI model optimized for detailed document retrieval), and Perplexity (specialized in concise, trustworthy answers from diverse internet sources), providing real-time and accurate results directly within consens.io.

7. ✅❌ What are the checkmarks and crosses? ＋

The checkmark (✅) selects an answer to be included in the consensus generation. The cross (❌) excludes an answer from influencing the final consensus.

8. 🔑 When do I need to enter API keys? ＋

You only need to enter your own API keys if you're not logged in or wish to use your personal model access.

9. 🧠 Which AI models are available? ＋

consens.io provides access to top-tier LLMs including OpenAI, Mistral, Anthropic, Gemini, Exa, Perplexity, and more.

10. 📌 How do Bookmarks & Leaderboards work? ＋

Logged-in users can bookmark queries for future reference, and view popular queries on leaderboards, comparing their results with other users.

11. 🔢 How many queries can I make? ＋

Free users can make up to 25 standard AI-powered queries (including consensus generations, web searches, and direct model prompts), plus 12 Deep Think queries. You can track your remaining quota in the sidebar.

12. 💸 Why are the first 25 queries free? ＋

We want you to explore consens.io with no commitment. That's why we offer 25 free standard queries and 12 Deep Think queries—so you can test features, compare models, and experience consensus answers without providing payment information.

This is still a work in progress. AI models can make mistakes.
