About consens.io

A small independent tool to compare AI models and highlight where they agree and where they don’t.

consens.io is not another chatbot, but a simple interface to send one prompt to several AI models at once, view their answers side by side and add a small “consensus” layer on top.

Back to App

What is consens.io?

consens.io is a web interface for comparing answers from different large language models. You enter a single prompt and receive multiple responses next to each other.

A separate analysis then tries to:

  • summarise the main points models agree on,
  • surface contradictions or blind spots,
  • give you a compact “consensus” answer as a starting point.

It is an experiment in transparency and robustness – useful for research, learning and everyday questions, but never a substitute for your own judgement.

How it works (in short)

  • You choose which models to query and adjust basic options.
  • The backend forwards your prompt to the selected providers.
  • Responses are rendered side by side for direct comparison.
  • A lightweight “consensus engine” highlights overlaps and differences.

Project & maintainer

consens.io is built and maintained by a single independent developer with a background in data, automation and AI. It started as a private workflow for sanity-checking AI answers and gradually turned into a public side project.

There is no company behind it, no marketing team and no dedicated support department. Just one person running the infrastructure and iterating on the product when time and budget allow.

Data handling & privacy

consens.io aims to stay as minimal and privacy-conscious as reasonably possible:

  • Prompts are used to call the selected AI models and to display results to you.
  • Login and account features are handled via Firebase authentication.
  • If you use your own API keys, they are stored locally in your browser, not on the server.
  • There are no embedded ad-network tracking pixels.

For full details, please see the Privacy Notice. As a rule of thumb: avoid secrets, highly sensitive data or information that would normally require professional advice (e.g. legal, medical, financial).

Using consens.io

If consens.io is useful for you, you are welcome to keep using it quietly, share it with others, or send feedback via the button in the app – all of that helps to decide what to improve next.
